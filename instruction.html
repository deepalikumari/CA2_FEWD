Follow these rules and instructions to enjoy a challenging and entertaining game of Hangman on your computer or mobile device!

Rules:


  1. Word Selection: The game randomly selects a word from a predefined list or category.
  2. Hangman Figure: An empty gallows is displayed, representing the hangman. It has spaces for the head, body, arms, and legs.
  3. Guessing Letters: Players guess letters of the alphabet one at a time, trying to figure out the secret word.
  4. Incorrect Guesses: For each incorrect guess, a body part of the hangman is drawn. The sequence typically starts with the head, then the body, arms, and legs.
  5. Limited Guesses: Players have a limited number of guesses (6) before the hangman is complete.
  6. Winning or Losing: The game ends when the players guess the word correctly before the hangman is complete. If the hangman is fully drawn before guessing the word, the player loses.

Instructions:


  1. Start the Game: Launch the Hangman game on your computer or mobile device.
  2. Word Selection: The computer selects a word from its database or category. This word is hidden from the player.
  3. Display the Hangman: An empty gallows or the beginning of a hangman figure is displayed on the screen.
  4. Guess a Letter: Enter a letter using the keyboard or touchscreen to guess which letters are in the word.
  5. Correct Guess: If the guessed letter is in the word, it is revealed in its correct position(s). Guess additional letters to uncover more of the word.
  6. Incorrect Guess: If the guessed letter is not in the word, a body part of the hangman is drawn on the gallows.
  7. Continue Guessing: Keep guessing letters until the word is revealed or until the hangman is complete.
  8. Win or Lose: If you guess the word correctly before the hangman is complete, you win and get 2 points. If the hangman is fully drawn before guessing the word, you lose and gets 0 points.
  9. Play Again: You can start a new game and play as many rounds of Hangman as you like.
